Key Analysis

Cleaned and analyzed retail transaction data

Identified top-performing products and categories

Analyzed sales and profit trends

Segmented customers to find high-value and at-risk groups

Key Insights

A small customer segment contributes a large share of revenue

High discounts often reduce overall profit

Some high-sales categories have low profit margins
